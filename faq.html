Visa Services

How long does Schengen Visa processing take?
Standard processing typically takes 15 calendar days from the date of submission at the VFS Global center. However, we recommend applying at least 45 days before your travel date to account for any seasonal delays or additional document requests.
Do you provide door-step visa document collection?
Yes, for our Premium Concierge clients in Delhi NCR, we offer secure doorstep document collection and return services for all major visa categories.

Forex & Currency

Can I get a multi-currency travel card?
Absolutely. We offer reloadable multi-currency cards that support up to 16 major currencies. You can manage balances, lock exchange rates, and track spending through our GoGlobal mobile app.

Travel Insurance

Is COVID-19 coverage included in travel insurance?
Most of our comprehensive plans now include COVID-19 related medical emergencies and trip cancellations. Please check the specific policy wording for coverage limits in your destination country.

Planning a Corporate Trip?

For business travelers, we offer dedicated account managers and priority visa processing. Our corporate FAQ covers GST invoicing and bulk travel management.
